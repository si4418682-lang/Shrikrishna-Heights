SPECIFICATIONS

Construction & Material Details

Built with quality materials and standard construction practices to ensure durability, safety, and long-term value.

  • Structure: RCC framed structure designed as per standard engineering norms.
  • Walls: External walls with weather-resistant finish; internal walls with smooth plaster.
  • Flooring: Vitrified tile flooring in living, dining, and bedrooms.
  • Kitchen: Provision for modular kitchen with granite platform and stainless-steel sink.
  • Doors & Windows: Quality wooden doors with aluminum / UPVC windows and clear glass.
  • Electrical: Concealed copper wiring with adequate power points and branded switches.
  • Plumbing: Concealed plumbing with standard quality fittings.
  • Painting: Interior walls with premium emulsion paint; exterior with weatherproof paint.
